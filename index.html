About Pooja Dance & Art Academy

Legacy of Excellence • Powered by a Professional Team

Pooja Dance & Art Academy, founded and led by Mrs. Pooja Rathi, is a trusted name in dance and art education with a legacy of over 25 years. With more than 40 years of teaching experience, Mrs. Pooja Rathi has dedicated her life to nurturing talent, discipline, and artistic excellence—especially among kids and ladies.

Over the years, the academy has grown into a professionally structured institute, supported by a team of certified and experienced instructors across multiple disciplines. We offer a perfect blend of classical foundations and modern training, ensuring holistic artistic development.

With offline classes in Nagpur and online programs serving students globally, the academy continues to make quality training accessible beyond boundaries—without compromising on standards.
