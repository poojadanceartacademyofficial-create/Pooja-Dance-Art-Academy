About Pooja Dance & Art Academy

Founded and led by Mrs. Pooja Rathi, Pooja Dance & Art Academy is a trusted institution in Nagpur with over 40 years of teaching experience and 25+ years of academy excellence. The academy focuses on disciplined training, cultural values, and professional artistic development for students of all ages.
