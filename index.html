About Pooja Dance & Art Academy

Legacy of Excellence • Powered by a Professional Team

Pooja Dance & Art Academy, founded and led by Mrs. Pooja Rathi, is a trusted name in dance and art education with a legacy of over 25 years. With more than 40 years of teaching experience, she has nurtured thousands of students with discipline, grace, and artistic values.

The academy is supported by a team of certified and experienced instructors across dance, art, yoga, zumba, and specialized age groups.

With offline classes in Nagpur and online training globally, we make quality education accessible beyond boundaries.
