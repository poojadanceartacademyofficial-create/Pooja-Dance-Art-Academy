About Pooja Dance & Art Academy

Pooja Dance & Art Academy is a trusted name in dance and art education, rooted in Nagpur and admired by students worldwide. Led by Mrs. Pooja Rathi, a certified Kathak Visharad with over 40 years of teaching experience, the academy has been nurturing talent for more than 25 years.

We believe that dance and art are not just hobbies, but powerful disciplines that build confidence, grace, and character. Our academy offers a balanced blend of classical and modern training, designed especially for kids and ladies, with both offline and online classes.

Since 2011, we have consistently organized annual stage shows, competitions, and cultural events, giving students real performance exposure and professional growth opportunities.
